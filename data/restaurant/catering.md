# Catering Services at Bella's Italian Restaurant

## Overview
Bring the authentic taste of Bella's to your next event! We specialize in Italian catering for weddings, corporate events, family gatherings, and special occasions of all sizes.

Whether you're planning an intimate dinner party or a grand celebration, our experienced catering team will work with you to create a memorable culinary experience featuring the same quality and authenticity that has made Bella's a local favorite since 1985.

## Catering Options

### Drop-Off Catering
Perfect for casual gatherings and office lunches (10-50 guests)

**What's Included:**
- Food delivered hot and ready to serve
- Serving utensils and basic setup
- Disposable serving ware (or upgrade to china)
- Clear heating and serving instructions

**Pricing:** Minimum order $200

**Ideal For:**
- Office meetings and lunches
- Casual family gatherings
- Small parties
- Game day celebrations

### Buffet Service
Ideal for events of 20-100 guests

**What's Included:**
- Full buffet setup with professional chafing dishes
- Our staff sets up and maintains food stations
- Service staff available for 2-3 hours
- Professional linens and serving ware
- Buffet attendant to keep food fresh and replenished

**Pricing:** Minimum order $500

**Ideal For:**
- Birthday parties
- Anniversary celebrations
- Corporate events
- Holiday parties
- Graduation celebrations

### Plated Service
Elegant full-service dining for weddings and formal events (20-200 guests)

**What's Included:**
- Professional waitstaff for full table service
- Multi-course plated meals
- Complete setup, service, and cleanup
- Premium china, glassware, and linens
- Bartending services available (additional fee)
- Event coordinator to manage timing and service

**Pricing:** Minimum order $1,500

**Ideal For:**
- Weddings and receptions
- Rehearsal dinners
- Formal corporate dinners
- Fundraising galas
- Award ceremonies

## Planning Your Event

### Booking Timeline
- **Small events (10-30 guests):** Book 4-8 weeks in advance
- **Medium events (30-75 guests):** Book 2-3 months in advance
- **Large events & weddings:** Book 3-6 months in advance
- **Last-minute events:** Contact us - we'll do our best to accommodate!

**Important Dates:**
- Deposit due at time of booking (25% of estimated total)
- Final guest count due 7 days before event
- Final menu selections due 10 days before event
- Final payment due 3 days before event

### Menu Tastings
Complimentary menu tasting available for events over $2,000. Sample your selected menu items and make adjustments. Tastings can accommodate up to 4 people and must be scheduled at least 3 weeks before your event.

### Delivery & Service Area
We deliver and cater within a 25-mile radius of our restaurant location.

**Fees:**
- **Delivery Fee:** $50-150 (based on distance and order size)
- **Setup Fee:** $100-300 (based on service level)
- **Service Staff:** $35 per hour per staff member (for buffet and plated service)

### Dietary Accommodations
We're happy to accommodate:
- Vegetarian and vegan diets
- Gluten-free requirements
- Kosher-style meals (with advance notice)
- Most food allergies and restrictions

Please inform us of any dietary needs at least 2 weeks before your event so we can prepare accordingly.

### Payment & Policies

**Deposit:**
- 25% non-refundable deposit required to secure your date
- Deposit applied to final bill

**Payment:**
- Final payment due 3 days before event
- We accept cash, credit cards, checks, and ACH transfers
- Payment plans available for weddings and large events

**Cancellation Policy:**
- More than 30 days out: 50% refund (minus deposit)
- 14-30 days out: 25% refund (minus deposit)
- Less than 14 days: No refund
- We understand emergencies happen - contact us to discuss

**Gratuity:**
- 18% service charge automatically added for full-service events
- Additional gratuity for exceptional service is always appreciated but never required

### Alcohol Service
If you're serving alcohol at your event:
- Must comply with local laws and venue policies
- We provide licensed and insured bartenders
- We reserve the right to refuse service to intoxicated guests
- Host bar or cash bar options available
