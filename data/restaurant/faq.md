# Bella's Italian Restaurant - Frequently Asked Questions

## Hours & Location

### What are your hours?
We're open Tuesday through Sunday from 11:00 AM to 10:00 PM. We're closed on Mondays to give our team a well-deserved rest.

Friday hours are extended until 11:00 PM, and on Saturday we open at 10:00 AM for brunch. Sunday we're open 10:00 AM to 9:00 PM.

### Where are you located?
123 Main Street, Downtown, CA 90210. We're right across from City Hall, near the public parking garage.

### Is parking available?
Yes! We validate parking for the Main Street Garage (2 hours free with dining). Street parking is also available, and there's a free lot behind our building after 6 PM.

## Reservations

### Do I need a reservation?
While walk-ins are welcome, we highly recommend reservations, especially for Friday and Saturday evenings. You can book online, call us, or use our chat assistant!

### How far in advance can I book?
Up to 60 days in advance. For large parties (8+) or special events, please call us directly at (555) 123-4567.

### What's your cancellation policy?
We understand plans change! Please cancel at least 2 hours before your reservation time. For parties of 8 or more, we require 24-hour notice.

### Can I make changes to my reservation?
Absolutely! Just call us at (555) 123-4567 with your confirmation number and we'll be happy to help.

## Dietary Accommodations

### Do you have vegetarian options?
Absolutely! We have many vegetarian pasta dishes, pizzas, and entrees. Just look for the ðŸŒ± symbol on our menu. Popular choices include Fettuccine Alfredo, Margherita Pizza, and Melanzane alla Parmigiana.

### What about vegan options?
Yes, we offer several vegan dishes and can modify many items. Our Penne Arrabbiata and Marinara Pizza are naturally vegan, and our chefs are happy to accommodate. Just let your server know.

### Can you accommodate food allergies?
Yes, we take allergies very seriously. Please inform us of any allergies when you book or when you arrive. Our kitchen staff is trained to handle common allergens including gluten, nuts, dairy, shellfish, eggs, and soy.

### Do you have gluten-free pasta?
Yes! We offer gluten-free pasta for most of our pasta dishes at no extra charge. We also have gluten-free pizza crust available.

### Are your gluten-free options prepared separately?
While we take precautions to avoid cross-contamination, our kitchen is not a dedicated gluten-free facility. Please let us know if you have celiac disease so we can take extra care.

## Dining Options

### Do you offer takeout?
Yes, our full menu is available for takeout. Call ahead at (555) 123-4567 or order through our website for faster service.

### Do you deliver?
We partner with DoorDash for delivery within a 5-mile radius. You can also pick up curbside if you prefer.

### Do you have outdoor seating?
Yes! Our patio seats 20 guests and is open weather permitting from April through October. It's first-come, first-served for walk-ins, but you can request patio seating when making reservations (subject to availability).

### Is the restaurant kid-friendly?
Absolutely! We're a family restaurant and love having young guests. We have high chairs, booster seats, and a kids menu. Our staff is experienced with families.

### Do you have a kids menu?
Yes! We offer kid-sized portions of pasta, pizza, and chicken dishes, all under $10.

## Special Services

### Do you cater events?
Yes! We cater weddings, corporate events, parties, and more. We offer drop-off catering, buffet service, and full plated service. Contact our catering team at catering@bellasitalian.com or (555) 123-4568.

### Can I host a private event?
Yes, our private dining room seats up to 40 guests for intimate gatherings. We also offer full restaurant buyouts for larger events (up to 120 guests). Contact us at events@bellasitalian.com to discuss your event.

### Do you host rehearsal dinners?
Yes! Our private dining room is perfect for rehearsal dinners. We can create custom menus for your special occasion.

### Do you have gift cards?
Yes, available in any denomination. Purchase in-restaurant or online at www.bellasitalian.com. They never expire and make perfect gifts!

## Food & Beverage

### Do you make your pasta in-house?
Yes! All our pasta is made fresh daily using traditional Italian techniques and imported Italian flour. You can taste the difference!

### Where do you source your ingredients?
We source locally whenever possible for freshness and support local farmers. We also import key ingredients like olive oil, San Marzano tomatoes, and authentic Italian cheeses directly from Italy.

### Do you have a full bar?
Yes! We offer an extensive Italian wine list featuring wines from every major Italian region, craft cocktails with an Italian twist, Italian beers, and non-alcoholic beverages.

### Can I bring my own wine?
We allow BYOW (Bring Your Own Wine) with a $25 corkage fee per bottle. We have a limit of 2 bottles per party. Our sommelier is happy to help if you need wine recommendations from our list.

### Do you offer wine tastings?
Yes! We host wine tasting events quarterly, featuring Italian wines paired with small plates. Check our website or ask your server for upcoming dates.

### Can I order half portions?
For some pasta dishes, we can prepare half portions for a reduced price. Please ask your server.

## Policies

### What's your dress code?
Casual to business casual. We want you to be comfortable! We ask that guests avoid beachwear or athletic attire at dinner service.

### Are you wheelchair accessible?
Yes, we're fully ADA compliant with a wheelchair accessible entrance, restrooms, and seating. Please let us know when booking if you need accessible seating.

### Do you allow dogs?
Service animals are always welcome. On our patio (weather permitting), well-behaved dogs are welcome and we'll even bring them water!

### Do you have WiFi?
Yes, free WiFi is available for all guests. Ask your server for the password.

### Can I take photos?
Of course! We love when guests share their experiences. Tag us @bellasitalian on Instagram!

### Is there a time limit on tables?
For regular reservations, we allow 2 hours for parties up to 4 people, and 2.5 hours for larger groups. If the restaurant isn't busy, you're welcome to stay longer.
