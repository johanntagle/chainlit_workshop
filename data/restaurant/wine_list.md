# Wine List - Bella's Italian Restaurant

*Curated by our sommelier to complement our authentic Italian cuisine*

---

## White Wines

### Pinot Grigio

**Santa Margherita, Alto Adige** - $42 bottle / $12 glass
Crisp and light-bodied with notes of citrus, green apple, and white flowers. Clean, refreshing finish.

*Pairs beautifully with: Seafood pasta, light appetizers, grilled fish, salads*

---

**Livio Felluga, Friuli** - $38 bottle / $10 glass
Mineral-driven with white flowers and stone fruit. More complex than typical Pinot Grigio with excellent structure.

*Pairs beautifully with: Linguine alle Vongole, oysters, grilled branzino*

---

### Chardonnay

**Gaja Rossj-Bass, Piedmont** - $65 bottle
Rich and elegant with notes of butterscotch, vanilla, and ripe pear. Aged in oak for complexity without being overpowering.

*Pairs beautifully with: Chicken Marsala, creamy pasta dishes, lobster*

---

**Planeta, Sicily** - $45 bottle / $12 glass
Tropical fruit notes with crisp acidity. Less oak influence, more fruit-forward style.

*Pairs beautifully with: Fettuccine Alfredo, grilled chicken, creamy risotto*

---

### Vermentino

**Argiolas Costamolino, Sardinia** - $35 bottle / $10 glass
Fresh, herbal, and Mediterranean in character. Notes of citrus, white peach, and sea salt minerality.

*Pairs beautifully with: Seafood, light pasta, fried calamari, summer dishes*

---

**Antinori, Bolgheri** - $48 bottle
Premium Vermentino with more depth and complexity. Floral aromatics with stone fruit and almond notes.

*Pairs beautifully with: Branzino, shrimp dishes, grilled vegetables*

---

### Sauvignon Blanc

**Pieropan, Veneto** - $40 bottle
Bright acidity with grapefruit, herbs, and passion fruit. Crisp and refreshing.

*Pairs beautifully with: Salads, fresh mozzarella, tomato-based dishes, goat cheese*

---

### Soave

**Pieropan "La Rocca", Veneto** - $52 bottle
Made from Garganega grapes. Delicate with white flowers, almond, and citrus. Elegant and age-worthy.

*Pairs beautifully with: Seafood risotto, white fish, light pasta dishes*

---

## Red Wines

### Chianti

**Castello di Ama, Tuscany DOCG** - $48 bottle
Classic Tuscan Chianti - cherry, earth, and firm tannins with bright acidity. Food-friendly and authentic.

*Pairs beautifully with: Red sauce pasta, pizza, tomato-based dishes, aged cheese*

---

**Antinori Pèppoli, Tuscany** - $38 bottle / $11 glass
Bright fruit, medium body, very approachable. Cherry, violet, and spice notes.

*Pairs beautifully with: Spaghetti with meatballs, Margherita pizza, Bolognese*

---

**Ruffino Riserva Ducale, Tuscany** - $55 bottle
More structured Chianti with aging potential. Dark cherry, leather, and tobacco notes.

*Pairs beautifully with: Grilled meats, Bistecca Fiorentina, aged parmesan*

---

### Barbera

**Michele Chiarlo, Piedmont** - $42 bottle
Juicy with low tannins and high acidity. Red cherry, plum, and spice. Very food-friendly.

*Pairs beautifully with: Rich meat sauces, Osso Buco, mushroom dishes*

---

**Vietti "Tre Vigne", Piedmont** - $58 bottle
Premium Barbera with more concentration. Ripe fruit with hints of vanilla from oak aging.

*Pairs beautifully with: Braised meats, veal dishes, truffle pasta*

---

### Montepulciano d'Abruzzo

**Masciarelli, Abruzzo** - $32 bottle / $9 glass
Rustic, hearty, dark fruit character. Cherry, plum, and spice with moderate tannins.

*Pairs beautifully with: Lamb, beef, hearty pasta, pizza*

---

**Emidio Pepe, Abruzzo** - $75 bottle
Artisanal producer using organic methods. More refined and complex with excellent structure.

*Pairs beautifully with: Grilled meats, game, aged cheese*

---

### Barolo

**Paolo Scavino, Piedmont DOCG** - $95 bottle
"The King of Wines" - powerful, complex, age-worthy. Rose petal, cherry, tar, and earth. Requires breathing time.

*Pairs beautifully with: Braised meats, truffle dishes, aged cheese, Osso Buco*

---

**Borgogno, Piedmont DOCG** - $110 bottle
Traditional style Barolo with firm tannins and classic tar and rose aromatics. Exceptional aging potential.

*Pairs beautifully with: Wild game, Brasato al Barolo, mushroom risotto*

---

### Amarone della Valpolicella

**Tommasi, Veneto** - $85 bottle
Rich, full-bodied with dried fruit character (raisins, fig). Made from partially dried grapes. Sweet tannins and long finish.

*Pairs beautifully with: Beef, strong cheeses (gorgonzola), chocolate desserts*

---

**Allegrini, Veneto** - $120 bottle
Premium Amarone with more elegance and complexity. Dried cherry, spice, and chocolate notes.

*Pairs beautifully with: Osso Buco, venison, dark chocolate torte*

---

### Brunello di Montalcino

**Banfi, Tuscany DOCG** - $95 bottle
Made from 100% Sangiovese. Full-bodied with cherry, leather, and earth. Structured with firm tannins.

*Pairs beautifully with: Bistecca Fiorentina, wild boar, aged pecorino*

---

### Super Tuscan

**Tignanello, Antinori** - $120 bottle
Iconic blend of Sangiovese and Cabernet. Bold, elegant, complex. Dark fruit, vanilla, and spice.

*Pairs beautifully with: Steaks, game meats, lamb, rich pasta dishes*

---

**Sassicaia, Tenuta San Guido** - $250 bottle
One of Italy's most prestigious wines. Cabernet-based blend with incredible depth and aging potential.

*Pairs beautifully with: Prime cuts of beef, special occasions*

---

### Nero d'Avola

**Planeta, Sicily** - $38 bottle / $11 glass
Sicily's signature grape. Medium to full-bodied with black cherry, plum, and spice.

*Pairs beautifully with: Pizza, grilled meats, eggplant parmigiana*

## Wine by the Glass

All of our by-the-glass selections are available in 6oz pours. We also offer 3oz "taste" pours at half price - perfect for trying something new!

## Large Format Bottles

Ask your server about our selection of magnums (1.5L) and larger formats for special celebrations!

## Corkage Policy

We welcome you to bring your own wine with a $25 corkage fee per bottle (limit 2 bottles per party). Our sommelier is happy to provide glassware and decanting service.

## Wine Storage & Service

All our wines are stored in temperature-controlled conditions. Red wines are served at proper cellar temperature (60-65°F). We're happy to decant older wines or provide an ice bucket for whites upon request.
